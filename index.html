People often make prejudgments about people’s personalities and manners based on the way they speak, specifically when they talk in their native dialects. The minute we speak, we are giving clues about ourself — where we grew up, with whom we grew up, and where we went to school.Languages possess the power to shape narratives and build atmospheres within groups and communities.

This website will showcase audios from different speakers, where it invites you to form ideas about them when you hear their voices.
