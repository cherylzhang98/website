Joan Chen

Joan Chen is a Chinese actress, film director, screenwriter, and film producer. In China she performed in the 1979 film Little Flower and came to the attention of western audiences for her performance in the 1987 film The Last Emperor.

Born: April 26, 1961, Shanghai, China

Education: Shanghai Film Academy and the Shanghai Institute of Foreign Languages, State University of New York and California State University at Northridge.
